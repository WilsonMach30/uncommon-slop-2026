## Conversation Blueprint for Wafer AI

### 1. Image Analysis
The image shows a takeout meal from Panda Express Chinese restaurant featuring orange chicken, beef and broccoli, fried rice with mixed vegetables, lo mein noodles, served with a red drink cup and fortune cookie. The meal is presented in a white styrofoam container on a wooden slatted surface.

### 2. Target Vocabulary (3 words)
- **pollo agridulce** (sweet and sour chicken/orange chicken)
- **fideos** (noodles) 
- **brócoli** (broccoli)

### 3. Primary Grammatical Focus
**Ser vs. Estar usage** - Since David confuses these verbs when talking about locations, practice should focus on:
- "La comida está en el recipiente" (food IS LOCATED in the container)
- "El restaurante está cerca de mi casa" (restaurant IS LOCATED near my house)
- vs. "Panda Express es un restaurante chino" (Panda Express IS a Chinese restaurant - permanent characteristic)

### 4. Conversational Opening Question
**"¡Qué rico se ve tu cena! ¿Dónde está ese restaurante Panda Express? ¿Está cerca de tu casa o tuviste que ir lejos para conseguir esta comida china?"**

*Translation: "How delicious your dinner looks! Where is that Panda Express restaurant? Is it close to your house or did you have to go far to get this Chinese food?"*

This opening naturally incorporates the ser/estar practice while building on his food-ordering experience and creates opportunity to discuss the specific dishes shown.
